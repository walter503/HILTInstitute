Ms. Louise A. Vogel

Louise A. Vogel has been a language arts teacher since 1995. Ms. Vogel has spent time in Europe and Mexico studying and teaching English, French, and Spanish. She earned a B.A, in French Literature and Education at Cedar Crest College in Allentown, PA while simultaneously earning a Diplome d'Etudes Francaises from the universite de Caen, France. She went on to earn a M.A. in French Literature from Pennsylvania State University and an M.Ed. from Marymount University in Arlingto, VA. She has received two scholarships from the Embassy of Spain to study at the Universidad de Salamanca and the Universidad de Zaragosa. she is certified to teach ESOL, English, and French. She has been teaching ESOL/HILT and English language arts at the HILT Institute since 2002.
